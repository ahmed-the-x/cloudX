Our Story

CloudX was created by a creative team, with a simple vision: file storage shouldn't be complicated. After years of dealing with overcomplicated platforms that seemed designed to confuse rather than help, the goal became obvious: build something better.

The result is CloudX, a minimalist approach to file storage that puts simplicity and user experience first. No unnecessary features, no confusing interfaces, just a straightforward way to store and access your files.

We believe the best technology is invisible technology. When you use CloudX, you're not fighting with the interface or hunting for features, you're simply getting things done.

Free Storage

We believe great file storage should be accessible to everyone. That's why CloudX offers a generous free plan, no credit card required, no hidden fees.

Security First

Built on zero trust architecture, CloudX assumes nothing and verifies everything. Your files are encrypted end-to-end with ultra fast uploads and downloads from anywhere in the world.

Built Different

While others add features, we remove frustration. Every design decision is made with one question: does this make file storage simpler? The answer shapes everything we build.
