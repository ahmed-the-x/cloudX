Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Here's how we protect it.

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

Our Core Principle: We can't see your files, we don't scan them, we don't sell them, and we don't share them. Your data is yours alone.

1. Introduction

Welcome to CloudX. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how CloudX ("we," "our," or "us") handles your information when you use our file storage service at cloudxhq.com.

CloudX uses server-side encryption to protect your files. Our mission is to protect your data from AI algorithms, machine learning systems, and unauthorized access.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (stored as a secure hash, never in plain text)

Cookies and Website Functionality

We use cookies solely for essential website functionality, including:

  • Maintaining your login session
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Ensuring secure access to your account

We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics.

File Metadata

We store basic metadata about your files such as:

  • File names
  • File sizes
  • Upload dates
  • Folder structure

Important: We do not store or have access to the actual contents of your files.

3. How We Handle Your Files

Encryption

All files uploaded to CloudX are encrypted server-side using AES-256 bit encryption immediately after upload. This means:

  • Your files are encrypted at rest on our servers
  • Files remain encrypted in storage
  • Server-side encryption protects against unauthorized server access
  • Your files are decrypted only during authorized download requests

Virus Scanning

The only scanning we perform on your files is a virus scan before upload to protect our servers and other users. After the virus scan is complete, your files are immediately encrypted. We do not:

  • Scan file contents for any other purpose
  • Use AI or machine learning on your files
  • Index file contents for search purposes
  • Analyze your files for advertising or data mining

4. What We Don't Do With Your Data

We are committed to the following principles:

  • We don't sell your data. Ever. Under any circumstances.
  • We don't share your data. Your files and information are never shared with third parties for marketing, analytics, or any other purpose.
  • We don't scan your files. Beyond the initial virus scan, we never examine file contents.
  • We don't use AI on your data. Your files are not used to train AI models or machine learning algorithms.
  • We don't track your behavior. We don't use analytics tools to monitor how you use our service.

5. Bandwidth and Speed Allocations

CloudX provides the following bandwidth allocations per plan tier:

  • Free Plan: 2 MB/s (16 Mbps)
  • Basic Plan: 4 MB/s (32 Mbps)
  • Plus Plan: 6 MB/s (48 Mbps)
  • Pro Plan: 10 MB/s (80 Mbps)

Note: These speeds apply to both uploads and downloads. Please ensure your internet connection speed is sufficient to take full advantage of your plan's allocated bandwidth. (1 MB/s = 8 Mbps)

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your files remain on our servers for as long as you maintain your account. You can delete files at any time, and they will be permanently removed from our servers.

If you close your account, all your files and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Service Continuity

Your subscription plans are ongoing and will continue until:

  • You cancel your subscription
  • CloudX ceases operations (with 30 days advance notice)

The Free Plan is free forever and requires no payment or credit card information.

8. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • AES-256 bit server-side encryption for all stored files
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Isolated storage infrastructure
  • Protected server access controls

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your account information at any time
  • Download all your files whenever you want
  • Delete your files or account at any time
  • Request information about how your data is handled
  • Opt out of any future non-essential communications

10. Legal Compliance

We may disclose information if required by law, such as in response to a valid court order or subpoena. We will comply with legal obligations while prioritizing user privacy to the extent permitted by law.

11. Children's Privacy

CloudX is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of CloudX after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through our contact page at cloudxhq.com/contact.

We are committed to addressing your concerns and maintaining your trust.

Remember: At CloudX, your privacy isn't a feature—it's our foundation. We built this service because we believe your files should belong to you, and only you.
